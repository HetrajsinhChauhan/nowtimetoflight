The Tradition

Pure Sattvik
For The Soul

To celebrate the birth of Lord Krishna, our culinary masters have curated a menu that adheres strictly to Vaishnava traditions. No onions, no garlic—just the purest ingredients sourced from organic farms.

We use slow-churned butter (Makhan), Kashmiri Saffron, and earthen pots for cooking to ensure every dish tells a story of devotion and authenticity.

Chef Aryan Sharma
Executive Chef

The Process

Culinary Devotion

01

Purity First

Every ingredient is sourced with intention. We use only first-press oils and organic dairy from local Gaushalas.

02

Slow Cooking

We utilize traditional methods like clay oven smoking (Dhungar) and slow-cooking (Dum) to extract deep flavors.

03

Divine Offering

Our plating is inspired by temple art. We believe food is Prasaad, making every plate a visual and spiritual masterpiece.
