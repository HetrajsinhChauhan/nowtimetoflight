Our Philosophy

Elevating Roots to
Global Standards

At Sattvik, we believe that vegetarian cuisine is not an alternative, but a primary choice for the refined palate. Our kitchen explores the depths of Indian culinary history, reviving lost recipes from royal kitchens and presenting them with contemporary finesse.

We source our spices from select estates in Kerala and our produce from organic farms in Gujarat, ensuring every dish tells a story of authenticity and purity.

The Process

Culinary Mastery

01

Ethical Sourcing

We partner directly with artisanal growers. From saffron harvested at dawn to farm-fresh dairy, every ingredient is traceable and pure.

02

Ancient Technique

We utilize traditional methods like clay oven smoking (Dhungar) and slow-cooking (Dum) to extract deep, complex flavors.

03

Modern Artistry

Our plating is inspired by modern art. We believe we eat with our eyes first, making every plate a visual masterpiece.
